What are NFTs

They are non-fungible tokens.

A non-fungible token is a unique digital identifier that is recorded on a blockchain, and is used to certify ownership and authenticity. It cannot be copied, substituted, or subdivided. The ownership of an NFT is recorded in the blockchain and can be transferred by the owner, allowing NFTs to be sold and traded. NFTs can be used for creation of digital collectibles and wearables as well as fractional token ownership.

Benefits of Non-fungible tokens

An NFT can be more efficiently and easily transferred among people around the globe.

What are the impotance of NFTs

One of the most important advantages of the NFTs is that the digital creators get to monetize their work which was difficult to do before. NFTs provide a way of creating verifiable ownership and scarcity, making digital art and other digital assets more collectible and valuable.
